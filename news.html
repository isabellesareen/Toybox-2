Spring Concert Series Announced

We're thrilled to unveil our spring concert lineup featuring world-class musicians and breathtaking performances in the heart of nature. This season promises to be one of our most exciting yet, with a diverse array of talented artists bringing their unique voices to Grey Towers National Historic Site.

The 2026 Spring Concert Series will kick off on March 5th with an extraordinary performance by the acclaimed Philadelphia Chamber Orchestra. Under the direction of maestro Jonathan Rodriguez, the orchestra will perform a stunning program featuring works by Mozart, Beethoven, and contemporary composer Jennifer Higdon.

A Season of Excellence

Our spring season includes eight spectacular performances spanning March through June. Each concert has been carefully curated to showcase the intersection of musical excellence and natural beauty that has become the hallmark of Kindred Spirits performances.

Notable highlights include a special Mother's Day concert featuring soprano Maria Vasquez, who will perform beloved operatic arias and Broadway classics. On April 15th, we'll host the internationally renowned Tak√°cs Quartet for an intimate chamber music evening that promises to be unforgettable.

Youth Education Initiative

In keeping with our commitment to arts education, each concert will be preceded by a free educational workshop for local students. These interactive sessions will give young people the opportunity to meet the performers, learn about their instruments, and discover the joy of classical music.

"We believe that exposing children to live music in such an inspiring setting can be transformative," says our Education Director, Michael Chen. "These workshops are designed to spark curiosity and create lifelong music lovers."

Tickets and Information

Season subscriptions are now available and offer the best value for attending multiple concerts. Individual tickets will go on sale February 1st. As always, a portion of proceeds from ticket sales supports our youth education programs and helps us maintain the beautiful grounds at Grey Towers.

For those who have never visited Grey Towers, you're in for a treat. The historic estate provides a magical backdrop for our performances, with concerts held both indoors in our intimate concert hall and outdoors on the terrace overlooking the Pocono Mountains.

We invite you to join us for what promises to be an extraordinary season of music, community, and natural beauty. Whether you're a longtime patron or attending your first Kindred Spirits event, we look forward to sharing these special moments with you.

Visit our website or call our box office at (570) 555-0123 to purchase tickets or learn more about this season's exciting lineup.
